title Using the rFactor 2 Pit Menu API

participant App
participant PM API
participant rF2 SMMP
participant rFactor

==Start up==
App->PM API:Connect
PM API->rF2 SMMP:(Connect)
==Selecting a tyre type==
App->PM API:startUsingPitMenu

PM API-->rF2 SMMP:
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:Display Pit Menu
App->PM API:GetTyreChangeCategories
PM API-->rF2 SMMP:
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu up/down and inc/dec)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
App<-PM API:"R TIRES:","F TIRES:"
App->PM API:GetTyreTypeNames
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu up/down and inc/dec)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
App<-PM API:blah Soft\nblah Hard etc.
App->PM API:translateTyreTypes
App<-PM API:Supersoft: blah Soft\nSoft: blah Soft\nMedium: blah Soft\nHard: blah Hard\nIntermediate: blah Wet\nWet: blah Wet\nMonsoon: blah Wet\n\nMaybe should be in app?
App->PM API:SetCategory(F TIRES)
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu up/down)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
App->PM API:SetTyreType(blah Soft)
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu inc/dec)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
App->PM API:SetCategory(R TIRES)
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu up/down)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
App->PM API:SetTyreType(blah Soft)
rF2 SMMP->rFactor:(Menu inc/dec)
PM API<--rF2 SMMP:Current menu lines
